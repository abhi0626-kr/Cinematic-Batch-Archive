FALL 2020

The Quiet Arrival

Empty hallways and digital greetings. We began our journey in a world that felt still, finding warmth in screens and small distanced circles.

SPRING 2022

The Great Awakening

The campus breathed again. Festivals, late-night diner runs, and the roar of the crowd at the stadium. We discovered what we'd been missing.

SUMMER 2024

The Final Bow

Caps in the air, tears in the library. A thousand days compressed into a single afternoon, leaving behind a legacy locked within these walls.
